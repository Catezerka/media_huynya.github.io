Television: The Dominant Medium

Public Broadcasting

NHK (Japan Broadcasting Corporation)

  • License fee funded
  • 600+ billion yen annual revenue
  • Political independence concerns
  • Two terrestrial channels + satellite

Commercial Networks

  • NTV - Yomiuri Group (Conservative)
  • TBS - Independent commercial
  • Fuji TV - Fujisankei Group
  • TV Asahi - Asahi Group (Liberal)
  • TV Tokyo - Business focused

All advertising-dependent

Political Parallelism in Japanese Media

Conservative

Yomiuri, Sankei, Fuji TV

  • Pro-LDP government
  • Constitutional revision support
  • Strong US alliance
  • Defense spending increases

Liberal

Asahi, Mainichi, TV Asahi

  • Anti-war constitution protection
  • Social equality advocacy
  • Environmental issues focus
  • Government oversight

Note: Social media platforms increasingly create echo chambers reinforcing these divisions
